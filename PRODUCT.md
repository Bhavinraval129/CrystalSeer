# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
- **Spiritual Wellness Enthusiasts**: Individuals seeking personal healing, energy alignment, meditation aids, and space cleansing.
- **Premium Collectors**: Buyers interested in rare, high-quality, ethically sourced crystals with natural structures.
- **General Seekers**: People experiencing stress, blockages, or transitional life phases looking for tarot/numerology guidance.

## Product Purpose
CRYSTALSEER offers a digital sanctuary to discover authentic, healer-activated, and moon-attuned crystals alongside spiritual counseling services. The goal is to provide a premium, trusted e-commerce experience that feels sacred and luxurious.

## Brand Personality
- **Voice**: Ethereal, luxurious, calming, and authoritative.
- **3-Word Personality**: Luxurious, Serene, Mystical.
- **Emotional Goals**: Induce feelings of peace, connection, and spiritual elevation.

## Anti-references
- Avoid the loud, over-saturated "deals and discounts" visual style of conventional e-commerce sites.
- Avoid low-contrast, thin gray fonts on white backgrounds that make information illegible.
- Avoid SaaS-style cards and repetitive icon grids that feel cold and technical rather than organic and sacred.

## Design Principles
- **Full-Bleed Aesthetic Focus**: Images take center stage, showing off the gorgeous crystals in their full glory, conveying natural luxury.
- **High-Contrast Clean Overlay**: Text is laid out directly on top of the background image using dark gradients to guarantee readability (contrast ≥4.5:1).
- **Tabbed Navigation Rhythm**: Navigation uses clean, full-width horizontal tabs at the bottom of the banner for an organized, interactive sliding experience.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- **Contrast**: Maintain a strict contrast ratio of at least 4.5:1 for all text.
- **Motion**: Respect the user's `@media (prefers-reduced-motion: reduce)` settings by using cross-fades or instant slide changes.
