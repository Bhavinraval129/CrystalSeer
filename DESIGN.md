# Design

## Theme
- **Ambient Aura**: Ethereal, luxurious, spiritual wellness.
- **Lighting**: Soft mystical glow washes, light lavender, gold, and deep plum.

## Colors
- **Deep Plum** (`#3b203b`): Primary brand ink, body text, and heavy backgrounds.
- **Primary Purple** (`#844c84`): Primary brand brand accent, highlights, active states.
- **Lavender Mist** (`#f8f1f8`): Soft base background, light borders.
- **Gold Accent** (`#bd8933`): Premium attunement badges, luxury dividers.
- **Soft Lilac** (`#f0e1f0`): Decorative borders and secondary highlights.

## Typography
- **Headings**: `Playfair Display` (serif) - Used for primary display elements, italicized keywords, and section headers to evoke luxury and timelessness.
- **Body & Controls**: `Inter` (sans-serif) - Clear, highly legible, modern sans-serif for UI labels, lists, descriptions, and buttons.

## Components
- **Full-Bleed Slider**:
  - Full-viewport width/height layout with background image `object-cover`.
  - Staggered Framer Motion slide-in entries.
  - Custom horizontal navigation tabs at the bottom with responsive flex row layout.
- **Glassmorphic Cards**:
  - `backdrop-blur-md` with semi-transparent borders.
